📖 Dhaba Onboarding Guide

🕹️

Movement & Controls

Use WASD / Arrow Keys or drag the Joystick anywhere on the screen to walk around the restaurant.

🍳

Cooking & Chefs

Stand next to preparation stations to cook manually (fills progress bar) or hire dedicated **Chefs** to automate it.

🍛

Serving & Buffers

Drop food onto Cashier counters or Dining tables. Seated customers consume matching food from the table stack.

🧼

Cleaning & Washing

Seated customers leave dirty empty plates. Take plates to the top-left washing machine to clean them and unlock seats.

🤖

Staff Automation

Stand on unlock circles to hire serving **Workers** who automatically deliver dishes, sweep plates, and collect tip drops!
